About Me

My name is Yolo Mzalisi, the photographer behind Yolo.FilmScapes. I am 19 years old and have a hobby of photography. Through my lens, I capture the breathtaking beauty of the world around us, as well as the joy and magic of life's special moments. I specialize in preserving memories of matric dances, birthday parties, weddings, and other unforgettable events.

I am a photographer with 1 year experience but willing to explore and grow as a Photographer, Editor and Videographer. As a Event , Fashion, Product and Landscape photographer.


My Experience

As a photographer and official intern at NuMetro Marketing, I had the opportunity to job shadow the production team at the Multichoice Fashion Show in South Africa, where I captured stunning images of the opulent designs and models on the catwalk, and later created a collage of my favorite shots to commemorate the graduation ceremony of our team, which was a highlight of my filming experience as a model for the event.
